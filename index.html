Smart Features

Discover how Revoliq's SmartCart technology transforms the shopping experience with its innovative features.

Built-in Barcode Scanner

Scan items directly in your cart with our high-precision scanner that automatically adds products to your virtual basket.

Weight Verification System

Our smart weight system ensures security by matching scanned items with their expected weight, preventing errors and theft.

Integrated Payment

Complete your shopping experience by paying directly at the cart using our secure payment terminal with multiple options.

Indoor Navigation

Find products easily with our smart navigation system that guides you through the store to your desired items.

Real-time Promotions

Get personalized deals and discounts displayed on our built-in screen as you shop through different departments.

Secure Shopping

Our multi-layer security systems protect your payment data and ensure a trusted shopping experience every time.

How It Works

Experience the simplicity of shopping with Revoliq's SmartCart technology in just three easy steps.

1

Grab Your SmartCart

Simply unlock a SmartCart with your app or loyalty card and start your shopping journey.

2

Shop & Scan

Add items to your cart and scan them with the built-in scanner. The weight system verifies each item automatically.

3

Pay & Go

Complete your payment directly at the cart and exit the store—no lines, no waiting!
